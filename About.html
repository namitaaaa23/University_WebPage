OUR CAMPUS

Eco-friendly 200-acre campus with smart classrooms, auditoriums, and seminar halls

OUR INFRASTRUCTURE:

JFN University has a spacious and eco-friendly campus equipped with smart classrooms, seminar halls, and auditoriums. Our laboratories are well-furnished with modern equipment to support research and practical learning. The central library provides thousands of books, journals, and digital resources. Separate hostels with Wi-Fi, dining facilities, and study areas ensure student comfort. The university also has sports grounds, a gym, and cultural activity spaces for overall growth. In addition, a healthcare center, 24×7 security, and high-speed internet create a safe and supportive environment.

Laboratories

The university has modern and well-equipped laboratories for Biotechnology, Engineering, and Computer Science. They are designed with advanced instruments to support hands-on training and research. Practical learning in labs helps students apply theoretical concepts effectively.

Library

The central library is a hub of knowledge with a vast collection of books, journals, and e-resources. It provides digital access, quiet study spaces, and research support for students and faculty. The library is open for extended hours to encourage learning and innovation.

Sports and Fitness

JFN University promotes a healthy and active lifestyle through its well-maintained sports and fitness facilities. The campus has playgrounds for cricket, football, and athletics, along with courts for basketball, tennis, and badminton. A fully equipped gym and indoor fitness center are available for students. Regular sports events and fitness programs are conducted to encourage participation and overall well-being.

Hostels

The university provides separate, well-furnished hostels for boys and girls with modern facilities. Hostels ensure a safe, secure, and comfortable living environment with 24x7 security. Nutritious food, Wi-Fi, study spaces, and recreation areas are available for students. The hostel atmosphere promotes discipline, cultural exchange, and overall personality development.
